Representatives of the royal house

Russian state was establish in 862. There were only two ruling dinasty in Russian history: Rurik dynasty and House of Romanov. House of Romanov ruled in Russia over then 300 years from 1613 to 1917. Their reign began after the suppression of the Rurik dynasty (1598) and the end of the Time of Troubles (1613) and had been continuing until the February revolution (1917)
