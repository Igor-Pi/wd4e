Wars of Romanov house

The territory of Russia has increased several times during the reign of the Romanovs. Main direction of expansion was principally to the east, north and south. But most of the wars, oddly enough, were fought along the western borders. Main opponents were Ottoman Empire, Swedish Empire, Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth, France
